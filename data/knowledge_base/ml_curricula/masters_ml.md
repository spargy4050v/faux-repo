# Masters in Machine Learning - 4 Semester Curriculum

**Level:** Masters  
**Duration:** 2 Years (4 Semesters)  
**Total Credits:** 60

## Overview
This comprehensive Masters program in Machine Learning provides advanced theoretical foundations and practical skills in modern ML techniques, deep learning, and AI applications. Students will gain expertise in developing, deploying, and optimizing machine learning systems for real-world problems.

## Semester 1 (15 Credits)

### Core Courses
- **ML501** - Advanced Machine Learning (3 credits)
  - Supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, ensemble methods
  - Prerequisites: None
  
- **ML502** - Deep Learning Fundamentals (3 credits)
  - Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, training techniques
  - Prerequisites: None
  
- **ML503** - Mathematical Foundations for ML (3 credits)
  - Linear algebra, probability, optimization, information theory
  - Prerequisites: None
  
- **CS504** - Advanced Algorithms (3 credits)
  - Algorithm design, complexity analysis, graph algorithms
  - Prerequisites: None

### Lab
- **ML505L** - ML Programming Lab (3 credits)
  - Python, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, PyTorch
  - Prerequisites: None

## Semester 2 (15 Credits)

### Core Courses
- **ML601** - Natural Language Processing (3 credits)
  - Text processing, transformers, BERT, GPT architectures
  - Prerequisites: ML502
  
- **ML602** - Computer Vision (3 credits)
  - Image processing, object detection, segmentation, GANs
  - Prerequisites: ML502
  
- **ML603** - Reinforcement Learning (3 credits)
  - MDPs, Q-learning, policy gradients, deep RL
  - Prerequisites: ML501

### Electives (Choose 2)
- **ML604** - Probabilistic Graphical Models (3 credits)
- **ML605** - Time Series Analysis (3 credits)
- **ML606** - Explainable AI (3 credits)

## Semester 3 (15 Credits)

### Advanced Courses
- **ML701** - MLOps and Production Systems (3 credits)
  - Model deployment, monitoring, CI/CD for ML
  - Prerequisites: ML501
  
- **ML702** - Advanced Deep Learning (3 credits)
  - Attention mechanisms, transformers, diffusion models
  - Prerequisites: ML502

### Specialization Electives (Choose 3)
- **ML703** - Generative AI (3 credits)
- **ML704** - Graph Neural Networks (3 credits)
- **ML705** - Federated Learning (3 credits)
- **ML706** - AutoML and Neural Architecture Search (3 credits)

## Semester 4 (15 Credits)

### Capstone
- **ML801** - Masters Thesis Part I (3 credits)
  - Research proposal and literature review
  - Prerequisites: Completion of 45 credits
  
- **ML802** - Masters Thesis Part II (3 credits)
  - Implementation and experimentation
  - Prerequisites: ML801
  
- **ML803** - Masters Thesis Part III (3 credits)
  - Analysis and dissertation writing
  - Prerequisites: ML802
  
- **ML804** - Research Methodology (3 credits)
  - Research design, experimentation, paper writing
  - Prerequisites: None

### Elective
- **ML805** - Ethics in AI (3 credits)
- **ML806** - Advanced Topics in ML (3 credits)

## Learning Outcomes
- Design and implement advanced ML algorithms
- Build production-ready deep learning systems
- Conduct independent ML research
- Deploy and maintain ML systems at scale
- Apply ML to solve complex real-world problems

## Career Paths
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Research Scientist
- AI Architect
- Data Scientist (Senior)
- ML Platform Engineer
